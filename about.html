INFINITE CONCURRENCY

Built for Massive Scale

The UHDC is engineered to adapt to your environment. For small-to-medium enterprises, the platform utilizes a highly efficient, flat-file JSON database and an SMB drop-box to track asset telemetry with zero infrastructure overhead.

For global, multi-domain forests exceeding 20,000 endpoints, the UHDC seamlessly pivots to a Direct-to-SQL telemetry pipeline. This enterprise architecture eliminates file-lock bottlenecks during morning logon storms, utilizing Active Directory Computer Accounts to authenticate directly to your SQL clusters for microsecond lookups at infinite scale.
